Privacy Policy for TripRescue

TripRescue helps users manage travel disruption claims and related documents.

The app may request access to your camera so you can take photos of travel documents, tickets, receipts, boarding passes, or other evidence needed for your claim.

We may collect information you provide inside the app, including claim details, uploaded documents, images, contact information, and trip information.

We use this information only to provide the app’s features, generate claim documents, improve the service, and support users.

We do not sell personal data.

Data may be processed using third-party services required to operate the app, such as hosting, database, authentication, analytics, or AI processing services.

Users can contact us to request deletion of their data.

Contact: support@triprescue.app
